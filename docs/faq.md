# FAQ

## Is this open source?
No. This repository contains **documentation only**. The implementation is private.

## Can I reproduce your results from this repo?
No. We share methodology and evidence, but we intentionally omit reproduction-grade details.

## Do you provide differential privacy guarantees?
Not in this documentation at this time.

## What modalities do you support?
Currently documented focus: **oncology-like cohorts** with **tabular baseline**, **time-to-event outcomes**, and **longitudinal ctDNA**.

## What do you share publicly?
Typically: aggregate validation metrics, methodology, and toy examples when appropriate.
