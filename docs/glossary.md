# Glossary

- **Synthetic data**: Artificially generated data intended to resemble properties of real data.
- **Utility**: How well synthetic data supports intended analyses/tasks.
- **Robustness**: Stability of quality under shifts (cohorts, missingness, schema changes).
- **De-identification**: Practical steps to reduce exposure of identifiable information (not a formal guarantee).
- **Disclosure risk**: Risk that synthetic data enables re-identification or inference about real individuals.
